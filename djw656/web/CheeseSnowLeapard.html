This is Snow Leopard.

- The snow leopard (Panthera uncia or Uncia uncia) is a critically endangered large cat species native to the mountains of Central Asia.
- It is known for its thick, white-ish fur and its ability to survive in harsh, snowy environments.
- Snow leopards are often considered the "king of the snow mountains" due to their solitary and elusive nature, as well as their adaptability to high-altitude habitats.
